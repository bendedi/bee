How all this works ?

What's Deep Learning ?

We have trained our neural network with a lot of pictures of bee and their coordinates. With that, the algorithm is able to know where will be the next bee it will see

How our tracking system works ?

Our algorithm will use the coordinates identified by our neural network to associate the coordinates of each image with the bee to which they correspond.

What kind of prediction can we make ?

We are able to count bee and detect mite like varroa and alert the user.
